Branding

The company sells a wide range of teas as well as teawares such as mugs, tea infuser, kettles, etc. They are a one stop shop for tea enthusiasts who are looking to expand their taste with selections of teas that are not normally sold in supermarkets. They provide detailed information on their teas which include caffeine levels, health benefits, ingredients and ways it can be made.
